Where coffee meets community

Thirsty ? Foxtail Coffee Co. is dedicated to responsibly sourcing some of the finest coffees from around the world. We, as a company, uphold our ethical and quality standards by making sure our environmentally-friendly farms are paid fair wages, so they may continue to grow exceptional crops. Our quality beans are roasted in-house and used in a variety of different brewing methods, shifting the way we think of coffee - one cup at a time.

THE WAY COFFEE SHOULD BE

Foxtail Coffee is unwavering in its commitment to ethically sourcing beans from across the globe.
